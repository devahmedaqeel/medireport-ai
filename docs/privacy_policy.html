Privacy Policy for MediReport AI

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

MediReport AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy, especially regarding sensitive health and medical data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Critical Safe Declaration: MediReport AI is an educational tool designed to assist with report interpretation. It does not provide medical diagnoses or treatment prescriptions. All analyses should be verified by a qualified doctor.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our report interpretation services, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: If you register an account, we collect your email address. If you use the App in Guest Mode, no personal identifier is required.
  • Medical and Health Data: We collect the medical report images you upload (e.g., blood test sheets) and extract biomarker values, units, reference ranges, and test names.
  • Device Information: We may collect standard operational logs (such as OS version and device model) to maintain App stability and troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data strictly to deliver the App's core features:

  • To extract biomarker values from uploaded report images using Optical Character Recognition (OCR).
  • To compare extracted results with printed reference ranges and supply official MedlinePlus educational explanations.
  • To display historical trends of your lab results in the App Dashboard.
  • To provide offline daily health tips.

3. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your health data extremely seriously:

  • All transmissions between the App and our backend API are encrypted over HTTPS.
  • Uploaded report images are processed transiently to extract text and are not retained on our servers longer than necessary to complete the analysis.
  • Biomarker records are stored securely in our database. Guest data remains local to your device using React Native AsyncStorage, except when syncing with remote storage under anonymous sessions.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health data. We do not share your health data with any third-party marketing networks or insurers. Data is only processed through trusted, compliant services (like Supabase for secure cloud synchronization and official NLM/MedlinePlus APIs for medical content references).

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your information:

  • Delete History: You can delete all your report scan history and biomarker trends directly from the App settings at any time. This action will permanently erase your data from our database and local device storage.
  • Account Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your account and associated email address by contacting support.
