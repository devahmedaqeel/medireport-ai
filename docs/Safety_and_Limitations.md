# Safety and Limitations

MediReport AI does not provide final diagnosis. It does not prescribe medicines, dosage, or treatment.

## Limitations
- OCR may misread blurry reports.
- Reference ranges vary by lab, age, sex, pregnancy and clinical context.
- Symptoms and medical history are required for diagnosis.
- Unknown report types may not be safely interpreted.

## Required Output Wording
Use "may indicate", "possible risk", "doctor confirmation required".
